TOKYO IS BORING

Come to Tokyo.
See the Shibuya scramble, see the sky tree.
Take a selfie, fast - the next tourists are waiting,
To take the same picture, and the next tourists too,
A clean system. Like a conveyor belt.
Just like Tokyo.
Tokyo people take the same path every day,
on trains,
buses,
cars,
to the same places.
You can see Tokyo, just like everyone else.
But,
We’ve got a new suggestion: Don’t visit Tokyo.
Tokyo is boring.
Take the other path. Visit Tokyo Handsome Boys.
