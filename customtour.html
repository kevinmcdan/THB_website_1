Make a new Tokyo Story

This tour is made fresh from scratch every time. In a before-tour questionnaire, you'll list the things you want to do, see, and eat in Tokyo. Then, we will hand-craft a tour based on your requests, and make a one-day plan featuring carefully selected spots which best suit your vision of an ideal trip.

Far removed from a plan found in a guidebook, our Custom Tour will feature only Real Tokyo elements for you to experience firsthand.

We don't go into the tour with preconceived opinions of what we want you to see; we hear what you want to see and pull it out from Tokyo itself. We believe that Tokyo will be able to handle whatever demands we throw at it. The Handsome Boys always go all in to make your dreams come true!!

EXAMPLE TOURS

Daytime

Let's head out and see Tokyo while you're most energetic! We can feel welcome everywhere, from the most well-known sites to the most local areas.

Cultural Experience

Kimono wearing, sushi making, fabric dyeing, even an aikido class; we'll make the arrangements for you to try a Japanese art form first-hand

OR

Imperial Gardens and Tokyo Station

From outside the beautiful palace of the emperor to the swarming city of towering office buildings

OR

Kiyosumi Park

Walk around and admire an essential among man-made parks

OR

Meiji Jinguu

The most famous and beautiful temple in the heart of the city

Afternoon

It's time to take a break after our exciting start. Let's go somewhere that's not overflowing with tourists, but instead a relaxed area real Tokyo people often enjoy.

Art Museum

Dive into the rich history of Japanese art, or be amazed at the strange and colorful beauty of modern styles

OR

Oedo Onsen

In the seaside town of Odaiba, wear a yukata and relax in a theme-park-esque hot spring

OR

Tempura

For lunch, we'll show you a local restaurant hidden but surprisingly close to the action

OR

Cafè

Take a break with fresh-roasted coffee carefully selected by the shopkeeper

Evening

For the people who haven't had enough of sightseeing, there's plenty more to do. If you've had enough of the big attractions, let's go local, or vice-versa. No amount of time in Tokyo is ever enough!

Shopping Spree

Visit a specific area to shop for world-famous Japanese cute fashion, high-class brands, cool hipster trends, and even otaku cosplay goods and collectables

OR

Asakusa and Sky Tree

Tradition and modernity collide; take a glimpse into Old Tokyo and a breathtaking view of New Tokyo

OR

Izakaya Districts

Grab a taste of local flavor still untouched by tourists just a little west of Shinjuku

OR

Maguro Mart

Straight from the fish: Now that's fresh!!

Night

Needless to say, Tokyo is fun at night. Leave the planning to us and you can't go wrong. Drinking, eating, karaoke, relaxing; it's all available, and it's all a blast.

Golden Gai

The tiniest bars in the world; step into the hottest downtown nightlife in Shinjuku

OR

Concert

We'll get the tickets to see one of your favorite Japanese bands! Local shows in tiny Tokyo venues also a must

OR

Traditional Bathhouse

Relax after a long day of travelling with a hot bath and a "kanpai" with a cool milk!

OR

Shinbashi

The classic izakaya style appeals to the salaryman eye; a playhouse for those in the offices near Tokyo Station

  • We can make reservations for the tour for restaurants, purchase tickets for concerts and events, etc. We cannot guarantee that these will be available on short notice, but we will do our best.
